History

TechCon began as a small local meetup for developers in 2015, driven by a shared passion for innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has evolved into a global technology conference, attracting thousands of participants from around the world. From its early days of intimate coding sessions to today's large-scale keynotes, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops, TechCon has grown into a dynamic platform that celebrates progress, learning, and the future of tech.

Mission

TechCon is driven by a clear mission: to foster innovation, collaboration, and inclusive growth in the tech world. The conference aims to bring together diverse voices to explore cutting-edge ideas, share knowledge, and spark meaningful connections. Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Patel

A respected AI researcher and advocate for ethical tech, Dr. Amina Patel has led groundbreaking work in machine learning transparency. At TechCon 2022, she shared insights on responsible AI design and the future of human-centered innovation.

Jason Kim

Jason Kim is a software engineer and open-source contributor best known for co-creating a lightweight web framework used by thousands of developers worldwide. He spoke at TechCon 2021 about building scalable web applications and empowering early-career devs.

Sylvia Muthoni

As the founder of Code for Change Africa, Sylvia has trained and mentored hundreds of young developers across the continent. At TechCon 2023, she delivered a keynote on tech education, accessibility, and digital inclusion.

Leo Tanaka

A pioneer in augmented reality (AR), Leo Tanaka introduced breakthrough AR tools for education and healthcare. His 2020 TechCon session explored how immersive tech is transforming the way we learn and connect.
